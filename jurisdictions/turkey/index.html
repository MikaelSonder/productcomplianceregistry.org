Executive Summary

Product compliance in Türkiye is the structured function through which products are reviewed against applicable Turkish technical regulations, product safety rules, CE marking alignment, conformity expectations and market surveillance exposure before and during placement on the market.

In practice, the subject is wider than CE marking alone because businesses must first determine which Turkish technical rules apply, whether the product falls under sector-specific regulation and how conformity should be demonstrated and documented before import, distribution or sale.

The Turkish system is shaped by the Law on Product Safety and Technical Regulations, sector-specific product rules, general product safety principles and a market surveillance architecture involving multiple authorities coordinated at national level.

Cross-border relevance is substantial because Türkiye has aligned many horizontal product safety and technical regulation elements with the European framework while still maintaining nationally administered responsibilities for economic operators and conformity assessment bodies.

The purpose of the product compliance function is to ensure that products placed on the Turkish market satisfy applicable technical and safety requirements and can be supplied with a defensible regulatory position.

It exists to convert product design, specifications, CE marking logic, conformity evidence, documentation and supply-chain coordination into a legally usable Turkish market-access position.

FAQ

The FAQ section collects recurring threshold questions in a concise handbook format.

Do Products Placed on the Turkish Market Need to Meet Turkish Technical Regulations?Yes. Products placed on the Turkish market must comply with applicable technical regulations.
Can a Product Be Subject to Market Surveillance in Türkiye?Yes. Any product placed on the Turkish market can be subject to market surveillance by the responsible authorities.
Who Is Responsible for Product Safety and Conformity?Economic operators placing products on the market are responsible for safety and conformity under the product safety and technical regulations framework.
Is Turkish Product Compliance Always Based on One General Law Alone?No. Sector-specific regulations are important, and the Law on Product Safety and Technical Regulations provides the general framework.
Can a Foreign Company Need Türkiye-Specific Compliance Planning?Yes. Foreign companies often need Türkish-specific conformity, CE alignment, documentation and market-surveillance planning.
